Make a list of business rules

Sessions

Are one hour long, twice a month, for 18 months (36 sessions max)

Doctor

  • Only see 20 patients each
  • Only be able to see their own patients in the study
  • Not know which drug they are giving
  • Observe patients and record data for the study
  • Record data per session with patients in the study
  • Make recommendation to whether a patient should or should not be a part of the study
  • Doctors will be able to edit/audit until study is complete (locked)

Patient:

Only 400 patients to start may be in the study

  • Patients will not know which drug they are taking
  • Patients fills out initial personal form for doctors (and supervisors)
  • Patients will offer times available for session via doctor's calendar
  • Patients can cancel 2 hours before session (time lock)
  • Patients can only be dropped from the study. No additional patients will be added once the study begins
  • After the study starts patients can only edit their future session time/date (-2hrs) and contact information
  • Patients can see personal information, sessions created and accompanying doctor's notes
  • Patients will be only be able to edit/audit contact information
  • Patient record will become 'dead', but information can still be read-only

Supervisor:

  • Supervisors will be the only people to know which drug is given to which patient
  • Supervisors will observe all data
  • Supervisors will make decision final of continuation of patients in the study
  • Supervisors will lock all data if a patient leaves the study
  • Supervisors will be able to put an edit/audit lock on the study for doctor's reports when the study is done
